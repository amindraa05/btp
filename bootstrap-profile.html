Copa Del Rey Trophy

The Campeonato de España–Copa de Su Majestad el Rey,[a] commonly known as Copa del Rey[b] or simply La Copa,[c] is an annual knockout football competition in Spanish football, organized by the Royal Spanish Football Federation. The competition was founded in 1903.
